About cryptoforharvey

Cryptoforharvey aims to allow people donate to harvey vicitims, and care with crypto currency. We accept, a hand full of crypto currencies, ETH, BTC, LTC, and soon more. Infact you do not even need to have crypto, or money in general. If you follow the directions below, we could use your computer/server/old computer to mine crypto. The funds raised from your computer's processing power will be given directly to hurricane harvey victim's care, shelter, and well being. Cryptoforharvey is completely anonymous, and very easy to donate. If you are going to directly donate crypto coins, and not mine use the addresses below.

Donating

By mining

Step 1

Download and install minergate for your operating system here.

Minergate

Step 2

Open Minergate. Under email type "crypto4harvey@protonmail.ch"

Step 3

Choose any of the coins (eg. XMR, AEON, ETH). From the dropdown, select the level of CPU, and GPU (if available). If you will be using the PC while mining, select 1 or 2.

Step 3

Leave the app running in the background, and for every minute you run it, you are donating a little more to harvey victims!
